Free Spotify Premium

A tutorial on how to get free Spotify Premium using root privileges on Android.

Prerequisites

  • Rooted Android device (tested with Magisk and KernelSU)
  • Zygisk must be enabled. You can use the ZygiskNext module for this.
  • Latest Spotify app installed from Play Store

Make sure you...

  • Logged into Spotify and played a song as a test
  • Have the LSPosed module and ReVanced Xposed APK installed
  • Follow the steps carefully

Steps

  1. Open LSPosed and go to the "Modules" section
  2. Enable the ReVanced Xposed module
  3. Tick the Spotify app
  4. Long press the Spotify app and select "Force Stop"
  5. Long press the Spotify app again and select "Launch"

Enjoy Your Free Spotify Premium!

Now you can enjoy Spotify Premium features for free. If you encounter any issues, make sure to check the prerequisites and steps again.
